Pete Lackey Says:

Why?

Simply because I believe the only appropriate response a creature can make to his Creator’s Word is full and faithful consecration to beliefs and behaviors consistent with that Word. And as priestly creatures endowed with the intellectual capacities to grasp that Word, the emotional capacities to desire it, the volitional capacities to choose it, the bodily capacities to implement it and the verbal capacities to say we’re determined to do all the above, it seems only fitting that we have some (even if only symbolic) way to express that. The act of ‘oblation’ lets me do so.
Step 6: Commit Your Day

Round out your reading of the Scriptures by praying to the Lord, “I hear You about _____ (something seen in this morning’s texts), and I devote myself anew to both You and that through the priestly gift of myself as a ‘living sacrifice’ available for Your use today ” or something comparable.
