Pete Lackey Says:

Why?

Simply because I want to order every belief and behavior by the ‘truth, the whole truth and nothing but the truth’ of God’s (entire) Word. This, I think, is a non-negotiable presupposition of priestly fidelity in the world. I read the Scriptures in light of my long-term goal of being sufficiently well-versed in God’s Word to be able {1} to aid others in understanding it—as a compassionate priestly interpreter and {2} to answer others who may be seeking to undermine it—as a competent priestly interpreter.
Step 5: Bible History

Read the chapters assigned to the day. These readings are chonological and if read daily will allow you to read the entire Bible in one year. Try to marinate your mind in the whole Word of God as a faithful priest listening intently to all God has said to men with an awareness that you've been called to witness to others on God’s behalf.
