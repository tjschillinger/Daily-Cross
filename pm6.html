Pete Lackey Says:

Why?

Simply because I wanted a more intentional (and repeatable) strategy for revisiting all of the major areas of life on a regular basis to prompt me to continually ask, “Am I walking in conformity to my priestly calling here? If not, how can I do so?” And this system seems to allow me to do that quite effectively.
Step 6: Offering

Take time to examine your life as an offering to God. There are 5 types of offerings throughout the year.5 types of Offerings:
{1} ‘Burnt’ Offering: Recurs Daily.
{2} ‘Grain’ Offering: Recurs Weekly (last day of each week)
{3} ‘Peace’ Offering: Recurs Monthly (last day of each month)
{4} ‘Sin’ Offering: Recurs Quarterly (last day of each quarter)
{4} ‘Sin’ Offering: Recurs Quarterly (last day of each quarter)Simply examine your life through the questions of each offering. If you feel convicted about something, ask forgiveness and commit yourself to rectifying it.
