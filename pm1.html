Pete Lackey Says:

Why?

Simply because I want to make a clear, verbal and intentional turn away from the priestly activities that have occupied me since the morning watch (i.e., serving men on God’s behalf) and toward the priestly activities that are about to occupy me in the evening watch (i.e., seeking God on men’s behalf) as I, like our first parents, appear for my afternoon/evening visit with our Creator in ‘the cool of the day.’
Step 1: Invitation

Simply pause, pray and appeal for the Spirit’s attendance in the activities that you're about to pursue.
