Pete Lackey Says:

Why? – Two reasons:

{1} I believe God delights to hear us enter His presence with song, especially since it calls upon every aspect of our humanity to sing (well), i.e., our minds, hearts, bodies, emotions, etc., and

{2} some of God’s greatest ‘gifts’ to the Church (cf. Eph. 4:10) came packaged as hymn writers. And I like to incorporate the theological and devotional insights of those faithful giants (from many different traditions) into my own worship.
