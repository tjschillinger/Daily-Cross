Why this step?

Simply because, as the term ‘invocation’ indicates, you should intend at this point to “petition for help or support” through a short “prayer of entreaty…at the beginning of [your]…worship” (cf. Merriam Webster’s Collegiate Dictionary [10th ed], s.v. invocation).

In other words, start your morning watch with appeal to heaven for help in hearing on earth.
Step 2: Invitation

Simply pause, pray, and appeal for the Spirit’s illuminating ministry in your morning reading/reflecting of the Scriptures.
