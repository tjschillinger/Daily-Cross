F.A.Q. Become a Sponsor

Pete Lackey Says:

Why? – Two reasons:

Simply because the proper disposition of a ‘priest’ before our God is one of worship. And worship seems most perfectly embodied in psalms/songs. But more, daily exposure to the Psalms trains me to ‘feel’ biblically. I find in this treasure of sanctified human passion a ‘voice’ for every mood I ever experience and a (general) model for every request I ever make to heaven.
Act 3: Doxological Reading

Read the Psalm/Song assigned to the day. This allows you to ‘join the chorus’ of the redeemed as one called to offer priestly praise on behalf of creation.
