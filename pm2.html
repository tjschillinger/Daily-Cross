Pete Lackey Says:

Why? – Two reasons:

Simply because I have three ‘doxological’ goals that this short exercise helps me reach each evening: {1} to reframe my mind after the events of the day; {2} to draw upon the riches of ‘devotional’ counsel scattered throughout the varied places and periods of Church history; and {3} to get ‘acquainted’ with the major ‘gifts of Christ to His Church’ (cf. Eph. 4:10) through their writings.
