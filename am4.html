Pete Lackey Says:

Why? – Two reasons:

Simply because I want to regulate my practical priestly life in the world with the very same wisdom that designed the world in the first place. And the best, short summary of that wisdom in propositional form is found, I believe, in the book of Proverbs. (The best summary of it in ‘personal form,’ by the way, is found, of course in Jesus of Nazareth. So I often try to see Solomon’s counsels fleshed out in David’s Son—if I can.)
